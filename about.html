Who We Are

Engineering Excellence, Personal Attention

CINTECH ENERGY LIMITED was founded by Akubue Festus Obinna, an Electrical Engineer with over 8 years of experience in telecom infrastructure, power systems, and solar energy.

After years as a Senior Specialist and TQM Auditor for IHS West Africa's largest telecom infrastructure company I made the decision to bring that same carrier grade standard directly to clients. No layers of management. No junior engineers. Just decades of combined field experience applied to every project.

My approach is simple: every site gets the same rigorous attention I gave to 100+ RFI upgrades and 150+ tower structural appraisals. Because in this industry, there's no substitute for experience.
